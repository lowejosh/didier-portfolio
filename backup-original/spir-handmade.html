SPIR HANDMADE

Spir was created to partner with private practices specialising in mental health. It is designed to directly provide their patients with products to aid their welbbeing with a heavy focus on the artwork. Each product is carefully designed to inspire the mind. All designs are printed on handmade-recycled paper.

The name "Spir" is the latin root word for "breathe". It is where we get words like respire, inspire, spirit & aspire just to name a few. All of which are key words that align with Spir's purpose to provide individuals with the tools for mental wellbeing.

PROJECT BACKGROUND

Spir is a design response to a brief asking students to create a product range with a specific audience. The purpose of this project is to take into account the environmental impact of the production process and prompt students to approach the creation of 3 products from an ethically conscious viewpoint.

CRAFTED PAPER

The paper used in this project was made from recycled scrap paper, sourced directly from the private practice where the final product will be sold. The scrap paper was broken down into pulp and meticulously handcrafted using traditional papermaking techniques.

BRANDMARK

The brandmark was designed to be a wordmark that can be used as a stamp on the paper itself. It was appropriate to have a mark that feels clean, healthy & professional as that is the environment that it will be seen in.

ARTWORK 01: KOI SWIMMING FOR INFINITY

My idea for this piece was to combine the Yin Yang symbol and an infinity loop. The Yin (dark) is represented by the black and red koi fish whilst the yang (light) is represented by the white and blue koi fish. The infinity loop was introduced to represent the infinite battle between light and dark in all of us (Infinte for our lifetime that is.)

ARTWORK 02: TREE OF LIFE GROWING OUT OF A HUMAN BRAIN

My intention for this piece was to symbolise the idea of journaling, to grow your mind. The tree of life is to symbolise the position we have all found ourselves in; reality, life, our human experience.

The human brain is to represent where this experience happens for us; consciousness, thoughts, memory and speech, arm and leg movements and the function of many organs within the body, our brain is responsible for all of these things

The design is off centered to account for the book binding, completed with both title of the product as well as Spir’s logo.

ARTWORK 03: Affirmation card with the moon

For the affirmation card I needed it to be simple, not only for its purpose but also because of the time restrictions. So I played on the idea from my second design, specifically the moon and the colour scheme. I wanted the focus to be drawn to the words of affirmation written on the back.
