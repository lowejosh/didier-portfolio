OSA

Oceanic Streamers Alliance needed a logo redesign for their twitch and social media platforms. The brief called for an abstract monogram logo that represents the region of the brand. The response was a fluid logo that play heavily into the Oceanic theme. This design is successful in representing the brand as it uses the acronym with water characteristics.

BRANDMARK

The main requirement for this project was a redesigned brandmark. The organisation was not happy with the current brandmark and wanted something that shows that it is an 'Oceanic' organisation. The idea was to maintain the O.S.A acronym whilst introducing water as the main design element.  

PROCESS

I first started with the acronym and broke down the letters into their basic shapes using circles. This way I have a clearer idea of how I am going to approach the logo. Then creating an abstract sketch to trace in illustrator

application

The application for the logo was important to show the client. At the time they were apart of a lot of streaming & esports events so it was important to design some apperal to showcase the brand.
