GAIA DISPLAY

A local vegan/vegetarian Cafe is becoming increasingly popular and looking to expand its business into a franchise. They want a font face that will suit their business, they’re after a decorative font designed for large scale advertising. The client has specified a detailed font that reflects the brand’s identity and values of quality plant-based foods.  The Intent is based on the proposition that; Nature Is Beautiful

To accomplish this, I am going for a serif that sits between a Humanist serif and a Modern serif. The beauty is from the Modern serif and the natural element is from the Humanist serif. If I am successful in Synthesizing these two typographic classifications I will be able to effectively achieve my design intent.

HER

Designed to be a caricature of nature, Gaia Display is an expressive font that sits harmoniously between a Modern and Humanist serif. Her high contrast, as well as a high angle of stress, allow this font to stand in a class of her own. She has beautiful curves along the stems which defy the structured appearance of most typefaces, this transitions nicely into the serifs.

The vegan cafe funding this endeavour have been happy to see how their new typeface has turned out. This will help them grow freely and beautifully as nature intended.

PROCESS

First starting with the sketches, I carefully crafted varients of each letterform until I built a consistent theme with consistent stress and contrast. Afterwards I constructed each letter in Adobe Illustrator then perfecting each part of type anatomy with controls until I achieved the final result.
