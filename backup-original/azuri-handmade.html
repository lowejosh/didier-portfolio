AZURI HANDMADE

Azuri handmade was a startup small business for handcrafted intuitive jewellery. The brief called for a brand identity consisting of a brandmark, graphic assets & a thank you card. Azuri’s core values—craftsmanship, storytelling, and a nomadic spirit—it was important to showcase these values in the final identity.

brandmark

Azuri’s mission is to show us all what it is to be human. The Wordmark is designed to evoke a feeling of wonder, connection, and unity, offering a reminder that humans are not separate from the universe but are a direct manifestation of its consciousness.

In essence, this logo serves as a symbolic reflection of the idea that we are the universe becoming aware of itself—a visual invitation to contemplate our place in the grand expanse of existence.

colours & uses

As the product is an intuitive process, the colour scheme reflects that by having many different colours, too many to showcase. The only defining factor for the colour palette is that they need to be pastelle colours, this way there is a loose theme and feeling between the brand and the colours. Pastelle colours also have a handmade and earthy feel to them, further reflecting the brand.
