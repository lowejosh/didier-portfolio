Lionheart

Brand identity for a company that assists adolescents with learning difficulties. Lionheart is an event for learning about inclusivity from industry leaders, designed for both students and teachers.

Year 2024

Mission & Vision

Lionheart was born of a parent not-for-profit organization that assists adolescents with learning difficulties. The organization needed a strong brand identity that would represent their values of courage, strength, and inclusivity.

This project was to design a brandmark for Lionheart, as well as other assets that would be needed for the organization to effectively communicate their mission of supporting young people through educational initiatives.

Brandmark Design

The main requirement for this project was a strong brandmark. The brief stated that it needed to be a combination mark of a pictorial and word mark, as it is more inclusive and easier to identify.

The pictorial mark was designed to encompass the brand name in a bold, strong & noble composition to reflect the core values of the organization. The lion symbolizes courage and strength, while the heart represents compassion and care.

Design Process

First starting with sketches, I quickly drew out some ideas on how to incorporate the lion & the heart together. After giving myself inspiration and a foundation to start from, I ideated several different marks to send to the founder.

The process involved multiple iterations, testing different approaches to balance the symbolic elements while maintaining clarity and impact across various applications.
