Brandmark Philosophy

Azuri's mission is to show us all what it is to be human. The wordmark is designed to evoke a feeling of wonder, connection, and unity, offering a reminder that humans are not separate from the universe but are a direct manifestation of its consciousness.

In essence, this logo serves as a symbolic reflection of the idea that we are the universe becoming aware of itselfâ€”a visual invitation to contemplate our place in the grand expanse of existence.

Colors & Applications

As the product is an intuitive process, the color scheme reflects that by having many different colors, too many to showcase. The only defining factor for the color palette is that they need to be pastel colors.

This way there is a loose theme and feeling between the brand and the colors. Pastel colors also have a handmade and earthy feel to them, further reflecting the brand's artisanal nature and connection to natural materials.

Simplified Applications

For smaller applications and digital use, a simplified version of the logo was created. This maintains the brand's essence while ensuring readability and impact across all touchpoints.

Thank You Card Design

This design was chosen to sit on the back of the thank you card. The card represents the personal touch and craftsmanship that Azuri brings to every piece of jewelry.

Each card accompanies handmade jewelry pieces, creating a meaningful connection between the artisan and the customer, reinforcing the brand's values of storytelling and human connection.

Graphic Assets

A comprehensive suite of graphic elements was developed to support the brand across various applications and communications.
