Brandmark Design

The main requirement for this project was a redesigned brandmark. The organization was not happy with the current brandmark and wanted something that shows that it is an 'Oceanic' organization.

The idea was to maintain the O.S.A acronym whilst introducing water as the main design element. This fluid approach creates a dynamic identity that represents both the gaming community and their oceanic heritage.

Design Process

I first started with the acronym and broke down the letters into their basic shapes using circles. This way I have a clearer idea of how I am going to approach the logo. Then creating an abstract sketch to trace in Illustrator.

Brand Applications

The application for the logo was important to show the client. At the time they were part of a lot of streaming & esports events so it was important to design some apparel to showcase the brand.
